BMW Series M3 Coupe

The BMW M3 is a high-performance version of the BMW 3 Series, developed by BMW's in-house motorsport division, BMW M GmbH. M3 models have been produced for every generation of 3 Series since the E30 M3 was introduced in 1986. The initial model was available in a coupé body style, with a convertible body style added soon after. M3 Sedans were available during the E36 (1994–1999) and E90 (2008–2012) generations.

BMW Series M8 Coupe

The BMW M8 Coupe combines breathtaking performance with luxurious design. Powered by a 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine, it delivers an astounding 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.0 seconds. The M8 Coupe's sleek, aerodynamic design is matched by a luxurious interior featuring Merino leather upholstery, carbon fiber accents, and a state-of-the-art Harman Kardon surround sound system. Advanced technology, including the iDrive 7.0 system and comprehensive safety features, ensures a connected and secure driving experience.
